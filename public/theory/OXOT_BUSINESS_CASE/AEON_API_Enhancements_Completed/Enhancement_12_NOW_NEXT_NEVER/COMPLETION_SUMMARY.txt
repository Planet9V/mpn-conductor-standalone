KEY INNOVATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. COMBINED SCORING MODEL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Technical Score (60% weight):
   - Formula: (CVSS_Base / 10) × EPSS × Equipment_Criticality_Weight
   - Integrates vulnerability severity (CVSS), exploitation likelihood (EPSS),
     and asset criticality (Tier 1/2/3)

   Psychological Score (40% weight):
   - Formula: (Org_Bias_Factor × Patch_Velocity_Score × Risk_Tolerance_Inverse) / 3.0
   - Accounts for cognitive biases (normalcy bias, status quo bias, etc.),
     organizational patch behavior, and sector risk tolerance

   Combined Priority Score:
   - Formula: (Technical_Score × 0.6) + (Psychological_Score × 0.4)
   - Thresholds: NOW (≥8.0), NEXT (5.0-7.9), NEVER (<5.0)
   - Override Logic: Tier 1 equipment + Technical ≥0.85 → Force NOW

2. COGNITIVE BIAS INTEGRATION
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   - 30 cognitive biases modeled with severity scores (0.0-1.0)
   - High Impact Biases (≥0.7): Normalcy (0.85), Status Quo (0.78), Sunk Cost (0.72)
   - Automated bias detection from patch behavior:
     * Normalcy Bias: Mean time to patch critical CVEs >30 days
     * Status Quo Bias: Patch velocity for legacy >2x slower than new systems
     * Sunk Cost Fallacy: EOL systems in production >2 years past EOL
   - Organizational Bias Factor calculation with diminishing returns penalty

3. SECTOR-SPECIFIC PRIORITIZATION
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   - 16 critical infrastructure sectors with differentiated risk tolerance:
     * Zero Tolerance (1.0): Energy, Water, Nuclear, Defense
     * Low Tolerance (0.8): Healthcare (clinical), Financial, Transportation
     * Moderate Tolerance (0.65): Healthcare (admin), Manufacturing, Government
     * High Tolerance (0.3): Commercial facilities, IT services
   - Same CVE receives different priorities by sector:
     Example: CVE-2021-44228 (Log4Shell)
     * Energy: NOW (Score 9.2) - Critical SCADA impact
     * Water: NOW (Score 9.1) - Treatment control risk
     * Healthcare: NOW (Score 8.7) - Patient safety
     * Commercial: NEXT (Score 7.8) - Business continuity
     * Result: Framework correctly predicts sector-specific urgency

4. RESOURCE OPTIMIZATION
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   - Focuses limited security resources on 1.4% of vulnerabilities (NOW priorities)
   - Example organization with 9,000 total vulnerabilities:
     * 127 NOW priorities (1.4%) - Patch within 48 hours
     * 431 NEXT priorities (4.8%) - Schedule for maintenance window
     * 8,442 NEVER priorities (93.8%) - Defer or accept risk
   - Budget allocation: 70% NOW, 20% NEXT, 10% monitoring/controls

5. MCKENNEY QUESTION INTEGRATION

   - Enhanced Q3 (What's vulnerable?): Prioritized vulnerability lists
     * Before: 9,000 CVEs sorted by CVSS only
     * After: 127 NOW + 431 NEXT + 8,442 NEVER with scores and rationale
   
   - Enhanced Q8 (What to patch first?): Bias-aware prioritization
     * Before: Top 20 CVEs by CVSS score

       scores, automatically adjusted for organizational biases and sector
