## Detailed Entries

### silence-001: Short Rest

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Stability
- Trait: Pause/Breath
- Strength: 0.7
- Description: Natural breathing pause in dialogue

**Theory**:
> Brief silence for natural phrasing. 
> Breathing room between thoughts.

**Examples**: Sentence break, Thought pause, Breath

## Silence Duration Guidelines

| Type | Duration | Beats (at 90 BPM) | Seconds |
|------|----------|-------------------|---------|
| Breath | 1 beat | 1 | ~0.67s |
| Phrase break | 2 beats | 2 | ~1.33s |
| Contemplation | 4+ beats | 4-8 | 2.7-5.3s |
| Fermata | Unmeasured | Variable | 3-10s |
| General Pause | All stop | 4-16 | 2.7-10.7s |

## Psychological Impact of Silence

| Duration | Psychological Effect |
|----------|---------------------|
| < 1s | Natural, unnoticed |
| 1-2s | Creates attention |
| 2-4s | Builds tension |
| 4-8s | Creates discomfort |
| > 8s | Maximum impact, rupture |
