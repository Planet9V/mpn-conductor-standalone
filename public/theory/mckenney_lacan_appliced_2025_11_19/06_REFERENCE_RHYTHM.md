### rhythm-001: 4/4 Time

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Entropy
- Condition: < 0.3
- Strength: 0.9
- Description: Stable, predictable rhythm for low entropy

**Theory**:

> The most common time signature creates expectations of order.
