## 3. Leitmotif Transformations

### melody-001: Prime (P)

**Definition**: Original theme statement

**Condition**: Normal state (τ < 0.5)

**Formula**: $P = \{n_1, n_2, ..., n_k\}$

### melody-002: Inversion (I)

**Definition**: Mirror around axis

**Condition**: Imaginary activation

**Formula**: 

$$
I(n) = 12 - n \pmod{12}
$$

Example: C-E-G → C-A♭-F

### melody-003: Fragmentation

**Definition**: Partial theme, broken

**Condition**: High trauma (τ > 0.8)

**Effect**: Only first 2-3 notes, silence, repeat
