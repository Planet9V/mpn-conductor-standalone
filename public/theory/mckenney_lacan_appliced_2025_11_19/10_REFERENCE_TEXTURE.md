## Orchestral Texture Entries

### texture-001: Monophonic Texture

**Musical Element**: Monophonic (single voice)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Relationship
- Trait: Isolation
- Strength: 0.9
- Description: Solo voice indicates isolated action or solitude

**Theory**:
> Single melodic line without accompaniment. 
> One voice = one perspective, isolation, solo action.

**Examples**: Soliloquy, Internal monologue, Isolation

**Reference**: RSCH-41

---

### texture-002: Homophonic Texture

**Musical Element**: Homophonic (melody + chords)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Relationship
- Trait: Group Consensus
- Strength: 0.85
- Description: Unified group moving together

**Theory**:
> One dominant melody supported by harmonic accompaniment. 
> Leader with followers = consensus, agreement.

**Examples**: Team alignment, Unified action, Agreement

**Reference**: RSCH-41

---

### texture-003: Polyphonic Texture

**Musical Element**: Polyphonic (multiple voices)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Relationship
- Trait: Competing Voices
- Strength: 0.9
- Description: Multiple independent voices in counterpoint

**Theory**:
> Multiple independent melodic lines interweaving. 
> Multiple equal voices = competing perspectives, debate.

**Examples**: Argument, Debate, Multiple agendas

**Reference**: RSCH-41

### texture-005: Ostinato Background

**Musical Element**: Ostinato (repeating pattern)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Cognitive Bias
- Trait: Obsession/Rumination
- Strength: 0.85
- Description: Repeating pattern indicates obsessive thinking
