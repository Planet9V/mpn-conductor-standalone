## Detailed Entries

### articulation-001: Legato

**Musical Element**: Legato (connected)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: BIG_FIVE
- Trait: Agreeableness
- Strength: 0.85
- Description: Smooth, diplomatic, connected speech

**Theory**:
> Smooth connection between notes with no breaks. 
> Connected = diplomatic, flowing, empathetic.

**Examples**: Diplomatic speech, Persuasion, Empathy

**Reference**: Canon Section 9

---

### articulation-002: Staccato

**Musical Element**: Staccato (detached)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: DISC
- Trait: D (Dominance)
- Strength: 0.85
- Description: Sharp, decisive, commanding speech

**Theory**:
> Short, separated notes with silence between. 
> Detached = decisive, commanding, efficient.

**Examples**: Orders, Commands, Decisive action

**Reference**: Canon Section 9

---

### articulation-003: Marcato

**Musical Element**: Marcato (emphatic)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: Emotion
- Trait: Emphasis/Assertion
- Strength: 0.9
- Description: Strong emphasis on each note

**Theory**:
> Each note strongly accented. 
> Emphatic = assertive, important, demanding attention.

**Examples**: Key pronouncements, Ultimatums, Declarations

**Reference**: Canon Section 9

---

### articulation-004: Tenuto

**Musical Element**: Tenuto (sustained)

**Psychometric Mapping**:
- Dimension: DISC
- Trait: C (Compliance)
- Strength: 0.8
- Description: Deliberate, thoughtful, considered

**Theory**:
> Notes held for full value with slight emphasis. 
> Deliberate = thoughtful, careful, analytical.

**Examples**: Careful explanation, Analysis, Consideration

**Reference**: Canon Section 9
