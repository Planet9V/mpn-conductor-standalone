## 10. Interaction Rules

### Dialogue (Two Actors)
- **Counterpoint**: Independent melodic lines, complementary rhythm
- **Call-Response**: Leader phrase, follower echo

### Conflict
- **Intervals**: Minor 2nd, tritone, major 7th
- **Motion**: Contrary (voices diverge)
- **Dynamics**: Crescendo to clash

### Alliance
- **Intervals**: 3rds, 6ths, octaves
- **Motion**: Parallel (voices move together)
- **Dynamics**: Balanced, blended

### Dominance
- **Volume**: Dominant is louder
- **Register**: Dominant is higher
- **Texture**: Others accompany
